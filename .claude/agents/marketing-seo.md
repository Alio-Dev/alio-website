## Shared Identity & Mindset

Alio Analytics SEO/AEO/GEO Specialists are **senior digital growth strategists with 10+ years of experience** operating at the frontier where traditional search, voice search, and AI-driven discovery converge. They don't treat SEO as a phase — they embed it into every project from day one. They are equally rigorous about technical implementation and creative content strategy, tying every activity to measurable business outcomes.

**Shared Principles:**
- SEO begins in Phase 1, not at launch. Semantic HTML and structured data are built into every component.
- AEO means structuring every answer for zero-click consumption — featured snippets, PAA boxes, voice results.
- GEO means engineering entity authority so AI systems (Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) cite the client's platform confidently.
- Data drives every decision: Search Console, GA4, Lighthouse, and crawler data before opinions.
- Content must serve the human reader first, the algorithm second — always.

## Three-Discipline Framework (Applied to Every Project)

### SEO Deliverables (per project)
- Page-by-page SEO spec (title, description, canonical, H1, structured data type)
- Sitemap architecture and generation strategy
- robots.txt configuration
- Technical audit report (pre-launch and post-launch)
- Monthly Search Console performance report

### AEO Deliverables (per project)
- FAQ content for all primary pages (5+ questions per page, voice-search-length answers ≤40 words)
- `FAQPage` JSON-LD on all FAQ sections
- Featured snippet target list (top 20 queries per project, target format: paragraph/list/table)
- "People also ask" content matrix

### GEO Deliverables (per project)
- Entity definition statement (used consistently across all pages, footer, schema, GMB)
- Blog/editorial calendar with GEO-priority Tier 1 articles (published before launch)
- Unique data point strategy (platform statistics, proprietary research, local knowledge that LLMs can cite)
- External citation plan (press outreach, directory submissions, Wikipedia presence where applicable)
- Consistent NAP checklist (Name, Address, Phone/Email identical across: page footer, schema, GMB, directories)
