Motion Design

Illustrations & Imagery

Illustration style
Geometric and diagrammatic — flat shapes, 2–2.5px line work, node dots echoing the logo. Palette limited to brand colors + one neutral. No mascots, no isometric people, no gradients beyond the brand pair. Illustrations explain (flows, systems, networks); they never decorate.
Photography
Client photo library — placeholder
Real Angolan professionals at work — natural light, honest environments (offices, field sites, server rooms in Luanda), candid over posed. Cool, slightly desaturated grade that lets brand cyan pop in UI overlays. Never: stock handshakes, blue-tinted circuit boards, generic skylines, staged diversity shots.
3D style
Used sparingly (hero moments only): soft-studio renders of abstract brand geometry — rounded glass/matte forms in indigo-cyan, single light source, generous negative space. Never literal objects (no 3D laptops, robots, or globes).
AI image generation — prompt template
Reusable base prompt; swap the [subject].
"[subject] in a modern Luanda office / field site, photographed on 35mm, natural window light, cool slightly desaturated color grade, deep indigo and teal accents, generous negative space, candid documentary style, no text, no logos — in the style of premium tech brand photography"
Negative prompt: stock photo look, handshake, circuit board overlay, blue glow, hologram, exaggerated smiles, clipart, 3D globe.

44×44px minimum on touch; 8px minimum between adjacent targets. Icon buttons get invisible padding to reach 44px even when drawn at 36px.
Form accessibility
Every input has a visible <label for> — placeholders never replace labels.
Required fields: asterisk + aria-required.
Errors: aria-describedby + aria-invalid, announced via live region — never color alone (icon + text always).
Groups use fieldset/legend; errors move focus to the first invalid field on submit.
